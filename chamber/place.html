Site Plan: East African Forests Explorer

Site Name

East African Forests Explorer (EAFE)

This name was selected because it clearly communicates the geographical focus and subject matter of the website. It's memorable, descriptive, and easy to understand for both local and international audiences.

Potential domain: eastafricanforests.org

Site Purpose

East African Forests Explorer serves as a comprehensive digital resource dedicated to the forests of East Africa. The website aims to:

  • Provide detailed information about the major forest ecosystems in East Africa
  • Showcase the unique biodiversity of these forests
  • Raise awareness about conservation efforts and challenges
  • Offer educational resources for students, researchers, and nature enthusiasts
  • Connect visitors with local conservation organizations and initiatives

Scenarios

"I'm planning a research trip to East Africa. Which forests have the highest biodiversity, and what permits do I need to conduct research there?"
"What are the main threats facing East African forests, and how can I contribute to conservation efforts?"

Color Schema

Primary Dark Green (#2C5530): Used for headings and primary elements

Sage Green (#8BA888): Used for secondary elements and highlights

Typography

Montserrat

Used for headings and navigation elements. Creates a modern, clean look while maintaining readability.

Open Sans

Used for body text and general content. Highly readable and works well across different screen sizes.
